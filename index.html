Due to the revamped design of 8tracks.com, it is normally not possible (at least not easily) to get a list of your favorite tracks. 8tracks Helper is a simple tool that fetches a list of all your favorite tracks on 8tracks.com so you can batch download or easily listen to them! Have fun!

Instructions: type in your (or someone else's) username in the textbox above; mine is "technix1".



What can I do with this? You can use SciLor's grooveshark downloader to batch download all your favorite tracks from an online music service called Grooveshark.
